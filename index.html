Neelam Valley

Biafo Glacier

The Biafo Glacier is a 67 km (42 mi) long glacier in the Karakoram Mountains of Gilgit Baltistan, Pakistan which meets the 49 km (30 mi) long Hispar Glacier at an altitude of 5,128 m (16,824 ft) at Hispar La (Pass) to create the world's longest glacial system outside the polar.

Pakistan Independance Day

Independance Day (Urdu: یوم آزادی‎; Yaum-e Āzādī), Celebrated yearly on 14 August, is a national occasion in Pakistan. It recognizes the day when Pakistan accomplished freedom and was announced a sovereign country taking after the end of the British Raj in 1947.
